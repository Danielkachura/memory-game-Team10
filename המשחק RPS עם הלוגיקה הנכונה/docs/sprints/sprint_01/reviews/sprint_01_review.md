# Sprint 01 - Review

## Review Purpose

Use this file at sprint close to capture:
- what shipped
- what slipped
- what blocked progress
- what to change in the next sprint

## Review Prompts

### Wins
- Which decisions helped move fastest?
- Which artifact was most useful during implementation?

### Problems
- Where did requirements remain ambiguous?
- Which bugs or failures took too long to catch?

### Process
- Did the CTO ownership model reduce confusion?
- Were DEV and QA handoffs explicit enough?

### Technical Debt
- What shortcuts were acceptable for the hackathon?
- What must be cleaned up immediately after the demo?
